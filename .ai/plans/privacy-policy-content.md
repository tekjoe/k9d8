```
This policy applies to information we collect:

accordance with this policy.
```

### 4. How We Share Your Information

**Purpose:** Be transparent about all data sharing.

#### 4.1 Information Shared with Other Users

```
By using k9d8, you choose to share certain information with other users:
```

```
- Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

```

```

- Sell your personal information to third parties
```

### 5. Data Retention

**Purpose:** Explain how long data is kept.

```
We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the 
purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period 
is required by law.

**Retention Periods:**

| Data Type | Retention Period | Deletion Trigger |
|-----------|------------------|------------------|
| Account information | Until account deletion | Account deletion request |
| Dog profiles | Until account deletion or manual deletion | Account deletion |
| Check-in history | 2 years from check-in date | Automatic after 2 years |
| Messages | Until account deletion | Account deletion |
| Photos | Until account deletion or manual deletion | Account deletion |
| Usage analytics | 26 months | Automatic expiration |
| Crash logs | 90 days | Automatic deletion |

**Deleted Account:**
When you delete your account:
- Your profile and personal information are deleted within 30 days
- Your content (check-ins, playdates) may be anonymized rather than deleted
- Some data may be retained in backups for up to 90 days
- Aggregated analytics data (without personal identifiers) may be retained
```

### 6. Your Privacy Rights

**Purpose:** Inform users of their rights under GDPR, CCPA, and other laws.

#### 6.1 Rights Overview

```
Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

**Right to Access**
Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

**Right to Rectification**
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

**Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**
Request deletion of your personal information.

**Right to Restrict Processing**
Request that we limit how we use your information.

**Right to Data Portability**
Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

**Right to Object**
Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for marketing.

**Right to Withdraw Consent**
Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time.

**Right to Non-Discrimination**
We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
```

#### 6.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

```
To exercise any of these rights:

1. **In-App:**
   - Access/Update: Settings > Profile
   - Delete Account: Settings > Account > Delete Account
   - Download Data: Settings > Privacy > Export Data

2. **Email Request:**
   Send a request to: privacy@k9d8.app
   Include: Your registered email address and specific request

3. **Response Time:**
   We will respond to all requests within 30 days.
   Complex requests may take up to 60 days (with notification).

4. **Verification:**
   We will verify your identity before processing requests to protect your privacy.
```

## Notes

### Key Principles

1. **Transparency:** Be clear about what data you collect and why
2. **Minimization:** Only collect data necessary for the service
3. **User Control:** Give users control over their data
4. **Security:** Protect data with appropriate measures
5. **Compliance:** Follow applicable laws and regulations

### When to Update

Update the privacy policy when:
- Adding new features that collect data
- Changing data processing practices
- Adding new third-party services
- Expanding to new jurisdictions
- Legal requirements change

### Formatting Tips

- Use clear, plain language (avoid legal jargon where possible)
- Use tables and bullet points for readability
- Include a "Last Updated" date
- Make it easy to find and navigate
- Ensure it's accessible (screen reader compatible)
