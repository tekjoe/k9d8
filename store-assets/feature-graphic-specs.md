# Feature Graphic Specifications

## Requirements
- **Size**: 1024 x 500 pixels
- **Format**: JPG or PNG (24-bit PNG recommended)
- **Safe Zone**: Keep text and important elements within 500px from the left edge (for different device sizes)

## Design Suggestions

### Option 1: Simple & Clean
- Background: Light gradient (light blue to white, matching app theme #E6F4FE)
- Left side: App icon or phone mockup showing the app
- Right side: Tagline text
- Bottom right: "Available on Google Play" badge (optional)

### Option 2: Lifestyle
- Background: Photo of dogs at a park (with blur/overlay for text readability)
- Foreground: App UI mockup floating
- Text: "Connect. Play. Repeat."

### Option 3: Feature-focused
- Background: Solid color or gradient
- 3-4 phone mockups showing different app screens
- Text: App name + "The social network for dog owners"

## Color Palette (from app)
- Primary: #3D8A5A (green)
- Background: #F5F4F1 (light gray)
- Accent: #E6F4FE (light blue)
- Text: #1A1918 (dark gray)

## Text Content
**Main headline**: k9d8
**Tagline**: The social network for dog owners
**Subtitle**: Discover parks • Arrange playdates • Connect with owners

## Tools to Create
1. **Canva** (free): Search "Google Play Feature Graphic" template
2. **Figma** (free): Create custom design
3. **Adobe Express** (free): Quick design tool
